About Our Team

ECLIPSA is a dynamic team consisting of two passionate engineering students: one specializing in IT engineering and the other in biomedical engineering. United by their fascination with space, spaceflight, life in space, and astronauts, they aim to leverage their diverse backgrounds to create innovative tools for visualizing complex biological experiments conducted in space.

Mission

To harness the power of technology and research to develop tools that enhance the understanding of biological experiments in space, making scientific discoveries accessible to everyone.

Vision

To be a leading force in the visualization of space-based biological research, bridging the gap between space science and everyday understanding.
