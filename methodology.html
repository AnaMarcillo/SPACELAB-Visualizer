ODD-665: Right extensor digitorum longus muscle

  • Description
  • Details
  • Methodology

This study focuses on the effects of spaceflight on ocular vasculature, which may be responsible for Spaceflight-associated neuro-ocular syndrome (SANS). The research involves assessing how microgravity impacts arteries, veins, and lymphatic structures.

Study Type: Spaceflight
Species: Mus musculus
Assay: RNA-Seq
Mission: RR-23
Transporter: Rodent Flight HARDWARE

Animal Care: 20 male mice, launched via SpaceX-21, kept in microgravity for 38 days.
Sample Collection: Tissues collected post-flight at TAMU, analyzed with RNA-Seq.
Data Analysis: ANOVA used to assess vascular reactivity and gene expression.

ODD-540: Unilateral lower limb suspension

  • Description
  • Details
  • Methodology

This experiment examines the effects of lower limb suspension on neuromuscular junctions and motor units in humans under simulated microgravity conditions.

Study Type: Ground Study
Species: Homo sapiens
Assay: Electrophysiology
Technique: Biopsy Analysis

Protocol: 20 participants, 30 days suspension.
Sample Collection: Biopsies from muscles pre- and post-suspension.
Data Analysis: Electrophysiological recordings and histology.
